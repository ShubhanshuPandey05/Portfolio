    ETHICS

    Accessible design

    A great website is designed for all people by
    default. That means considering people who use
    assistive technology and being mindful of
    everyone's ability to process information.

    Ethical design

    An ethical website prioritizes transparency over
    agenda. It clearly communicates the purpose,
    benefits and potential risks associated with your site.

    Sustainable design

    The web isn't green. Reducing how much data we
    store, how many emails we send, and how heavy
    our pages are serves people's experience as well
    as the planet.
